Sandstones

A study of their composition and texture assists to:

  • understand the environmental conditions in which they are formed
  • add valuable data towards predictions of their behaviour under well-defined climatic conditions

By means of thin section analysis and optical microscopy the precise composition of sandstone can be obtained

The shape of the grains can tell you something about the distance they have done.

In order to calculate the sphericity of a grain, several possibilities exist…

Grain shapes in Morpho+

Sphericity is a measure of how closely the grain shape approaches that of a sphere. One possibility for sphericity = ratio of the maximum opening over the equivalent diameter.

The shape of a grain is measured by calculating the long (L), intermediate (I) and short (S) axes.
